About Little Mix

Little Mix is a British girl band that was formed in 2011. It is the leading girl band worlwide with the best songs ever. Their songs are a mix of pop and R&B. They also combine other genres like latin pop. Over the years, they have won quite amazing awards and prizes owing to their hardwork.
Who wouldn't love to listen to songs that are actual masterpieces? They are the most charismatic group I know. Another thing, I also admire their zeal, passion, and tenacity to be able to handle challenges in the music industry. I love Little Mix

Band Members

Perrie Edwards

Perrie is an English singer and was born in 1993. She has been a member of the group ever sincce it started out. Her biggest achievement is being the first female ambassador for "Supreme Nutrition."

Jade Thirlwall

Jade is both a singer and song writer. She became a published songwriter in 2019 alongside Pinnock. Jade will be hosting an upcoming MTV web series, Served.

Leigh-Anne Pinnock

Pinnock is a songwriter, vocalist in Little Mix and an actress too. She turns out to have experienced alot of racism throughout her life. However, she has managed to be remain strong. She has helped writing a couple of songs for the band.

Jessy Nelson (Former member)

Jessica (Jessy) was born in 1991. Being a great singer, she joined the group while it was starting in 2011. She has been in the release of all the current six albums of the band. Saddening, Jessy announced her departure in the group.
In her message, she explained how hard it has been to live according to the society's expectations. And was leaving inorder that she can live her life without limitations.
