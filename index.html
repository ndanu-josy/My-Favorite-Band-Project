About Little Mix

Little Mix is a British girl band that was formed in 2011. It is the leading girl band worlwide with the best songs ever. Their songs are a mix of pop and R&B. They also combine other genres like latin pop. Over the years, they have won quite amazing awards and prizes owing to their hardwork.
Who wouldn't love to listen to songs that are actual masterpieces? They are the most charismatic group I know. Another thing, I also admire their zeal, passion, and tenacity to be able to handle challenges in the music industry. I love Little Mix
